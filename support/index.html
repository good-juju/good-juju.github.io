If your JUJU needs some troubleshooting love, please send us an email (help@sharethejuju.com) and hold tight! We know how fun life is when you’re creating and sharing JUJUs, so here are a few tips to try at home before clicking send on that email:

  1. Before installing JUJU on your iPhone, ensure you’re using the latest iOS 7 software (iOS 7.1.1)
  2. Art is downloading too slow? Make sure you have a strong internet connection.
  3. Frozen screen?
    1. Force close your JUJU App and reopen it.
    2. Restart your iPhone, by turning it on and off.
